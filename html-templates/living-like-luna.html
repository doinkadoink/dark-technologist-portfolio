LIVING LIKE LUNA

Student Film Production at Bond University

PROJECT OVERVIEW

"Living Like Luna" is a compelling student film produced at Bond University in 2022, where Rachel served as the film producer. This project showcases creative leadership, project management skills, and the ability to bring artistic visions to life through collaborative filmmaking. The film explores themes of identity, transformation, and the human experience through innovative storytelling and visual techniques.

FILM SYNOPSIS

"Living Like Luna" follows the journey of a young woman named Luna who discovers an extraordinary ability to see the world through different perspectives. As she navigates through various life experiences, Luna learns to embrace change and transformation, ultimately finding her true identity. The film combines elements of drama, fantasy, and coming-of-age storytelling, creating a unique narrative that resonates with audiences of all ages. Through innovative cinematography and creative direction, the film explores themes of self-discovery, resilience, and the power of perspective in shaping our reality.
