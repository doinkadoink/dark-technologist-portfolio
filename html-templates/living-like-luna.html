Project Overview

Living Like Luna represents the pinnacle of cinematic web design, drawing inspiration from classic film aesthetics and theatrical presentation. Using studio purple (#8a2be2) and film gold (#ffd700), this project creates an immersive experience that evokes the magic of cinema and storytelling.

The design incorporates letterbox layout simulation, subtitle-inspired body styling, and smooth crossfades between sections that create a seamless cinematic journey through the content, just like watching a carefully crafted film.

Project Highlights

Letterbox Layout

Implemented letterbox layout simulation that creates the authentic cinematic experience of watching a film with proper aspect ratios and framing.

Subtitle Styling

Created subtitle-inspired body styling that mimics the typography and presentation of professional film subtitles and credits.

Smooth Crossfades

Applied smooth crossfades between sections that create seamless transitions reminiscent of professional film editing and scene changes.

Scene Reveals

Designed scene-based scroll reveals that unveil content like dramatic film sequences, creating anticipation and visual storytelling.
