Alto Clef

~21 sec · First Maya animation · handmade character · SCP Foundation fan piece

FINAL ANIMATION

My first 3D animation: a character model I built in Maya, based on Alto Clef from the SCP Foundation — rigged, posed, and timed to music.

Music: uses the first 21 seconds of “BlackBoxWarrior — OKULTRA” by Will Wood. Character is a personal fan interpretation of Alto Clef; SCP Foundation content is used here as creative reference, not as an official work.

BEHIND THE SCENES

Breakdown of how the final render came together — from character modelling and animation to hand-made background assets, movement sync with the track, and a quirky AI voiceover of how I imagine Alto Clef might sound.

PROCESS

  • Hand-built Alto Clef character mesh and rig in Autodesk Maya
  • First animation pass — learning timing, poses, and camera read at ~21 seconds
  • Background set pieces modelled/assembled by hand for the final frame
  • Edits aligned to the opening energy of the Will Wood track
  • Presentation edit with process footage and experimental character voiceover
