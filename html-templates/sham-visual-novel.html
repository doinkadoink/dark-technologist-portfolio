OVERVIEW

Sham was an early interactive visual novel built in Ren'Py, made while learning to code and ship a first playable. The demo offers branching, comedic narrative around an improvised medical clinic — the itch.io listing describes scope, tone, and downloads.

Publishing under doinkadoink on itch.io; the build remains available for historical interest only.
