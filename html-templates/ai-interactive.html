CURATION AT SCALE

Running a creator account for years means treating each bot like a small product: clear voice, safe boundaries where the platform requires them, and steady iteration when user behaviour or model updates shift what “good” replies look like.

  • Long-form authorship — a multi-year arc of releases and revisions; learning which ideas sustain repeat play
  • Audience & iteration — public chats and likes surface what lands; feedback informs rewrites without losing each character’s core tone
  • Platform literacy — staying current with Character.AI tooling, moderation expectations, and how definition fields map to model behaviour
  • Community on-platform — followers and interaction counts accrue on Character.AI itself; the profile is the hub for that fanbase
