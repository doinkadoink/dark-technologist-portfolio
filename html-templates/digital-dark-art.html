WHAT IT IS

This page collects two threads of work: digital illustration (character pieces, surreal scenes, fantasy studies — mostly tablet and Photoshop) and photography pushed through Photoshop — composites, grading, effects, and hand-built elements on top of real captures.

Both sit alongside my broader print and publication practice (posters, magazines, layout in Photoshop, Illustrator, and InDesign) as experiments in colour, mood, and readable composition before ideas land in layout grids or brand systems.

Illustration thumbnails load from html-templates/rachelsartworks/ if you keep that folder next to this file. Photography pieces ship with this repo under html-templates/photography-art/.

PROCESS

  • Rough composition and pose in sketches, then clean line and value passes on the tablet
  • Colour keys and lighting experiments before committing to final rendering
  • For photo-based work: start from a solid exposure, build effects and composites in layers, then final grade for print or web
  • Export at sizes suitable for both web portfolio and potential print proofs when a piece feeds a poster or spread
