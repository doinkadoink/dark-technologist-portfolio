Project Overview

Digital Dark Art showcases a collection of hand-drawn digital artworks that embody the cyberpunk aesthetic and artistic rebellion. Using neon pink (#ff0080), cyber blue (#00ffff), and neon green (#00ff41), this project creates an immersive experience that celebrates human creativity in the digital age.

Each piece represents a fusion of traditional artistic techniques with modern digital tools, creating works that challenge conventional aesthetics and explore the darker, more rebellious aspects of digital art and cyberpunk culture.
