Night Kind City

2025 · Unity · VR walkthrough · personal study piece

WHAT THIS IS

A simple VR environment built in Unity (2025): a night–“kind” themed city and maze, a walkthrough through a purple forest and yellow-lit skyscrapers, plus lightweight coded vehicles to suggest traffic flow.

It is a showcase / prototype focused on mood, scale, and basic interactivity rather than a shipped product.

FOCUS

  • 3D blockout and atmosphere (forest vs. urban read)
  • VR locomotion and scene pacing for a short walkthrough
  • Simple traffic simulation (vehicle scripts)
