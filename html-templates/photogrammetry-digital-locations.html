on & on & on & on

~54 sec · 3 weeks · Scaniverse (iPhone) → Unreal Engine (PC) · home flythrough

Music: “on & on” by piri & Tommy Villiers (piri & tommy) — flythrough timed to the main chorus; edit loops start-to-finish.

WHAT IT IS

A 3D scan of my house captured on iPhone with Scaniverse, brought into Unreal Engine on PC for cleanup, then recorded as a cinematic flythrough through the interior.

The cut is built around the main chorus of the track — the piece is designed to loop seamlessly from the first frame to the last (~54 seconds), so it reads as one continuous pass rather than a hard cut.

WORKFLOW

CAPTURE

Room-by-room Scaniverse coverage on iPhone — planning paths so walls, floors, and furniture tie together with fewer holes.

UNREAL CLEANUP

Import and fix on PC: mesh cleanup, materials, lighting, and blocking a flythrough path that feels natural at house scale.

EDIT & LOOP

Record the flythrough in-engine, grade for readability, and align motion to the chorus so the loop point is invisible when it repeats.

BROADER PRACTICE

This sits in the same line of work as my other digital location experiments: real-world spatial capture as a foundation, then production-minded cleanup so environments hold up for lighting, continuity, and shot planning.
